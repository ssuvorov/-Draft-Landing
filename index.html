HIGH - END TECHNOLOGY

Smart soles from Senmotion

  • Pressure measurement

    15 sensors of sheer silk define exact plantar pressure distribution in a shoe and draw precise conclusion on your running technique..

  • Speedup

    Integrated sensors for speedup determine the exact foot position and efficiency of running.

  • Without any cables

    All the electronics are built in the sole. Data is transferred via Bluetooth 4.0.

  • Ultralight

    SenSoles are sturdy and at the same time so featherweight that a sportsman doesnâ€™t notice its presence.

  • Long service life

    You can easily take the upper coating of a sole off and put a new one. It makes it possible to use the soles for nearly unlimited time.

HOW IT WORKS

The concept of Senmotion

  • Making measurements while running

    The sensors in the sole make measurements of the biomechanical parameters of a sportsman like a data logger and transfer this information via Bluetooth to your Smartphone

  • Senmotion App

    Senmotion App evaluates the signals with the help of intelligent algorithms and creates both small reports of your running process and pieces of advice like those of a coach

  • Cloud storage

    All the data including RAW-data is transferred into a Cloud. In Senmotion portal you can see this data, share it with other people or use for any further analyses
